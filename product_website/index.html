Frequently asked questions

How portable is the Vortex Guru hardware?

Each tower ships in a protective flight case with quick-lock assembly. Typical setup takes under 15 minutes.

Can Vortex Guru operate offline?

Yes. Edge caching keeps critical assets available, while secure sync restores full functionality once online.

Which file formats do you support?

Upload OBJ, GLB, and point cloud volumes. Our pipeline optimizes them into proprietary voxel frames.

How do I integrate with existing systems?

Use our REST, gRPC, or WebSocket APIs. SDKs for Unity, Unreal, and Node streamline setup.
